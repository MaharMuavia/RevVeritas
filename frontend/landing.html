How it works

Deterministic detection + LLM judgment

①
Reconcile

Loads contracts, invoices and usage and lines up every disagreement across the three sources.

②
Detect

Exact pandas math finds candidate leaks and computes every dollar figure — never the model.

③
Judge

Gemini investigates each candidate with tools: a real leak, or explainable noise like a renegotiated price?

④
Recover

Drafts the correction or outreach behind a human-approval gate. Nothing is ever sent automatically.
